How to Use the Library System

1

Register

Create your student account by providing your details and uploading required documents (passport photo and ID card).

2

Wait for Approval

Your registration will be reviewed by the librarian. You'll receive approval notification via email.

3

Login & Book

Once approved, login with your roll number and password to access the seat booking system.

4

Select & Confirm

Choose your preferred date, time, duration, and seat. Confirm your booking and receive confirmation.

Library Rules

  • Maintain complete silence in study areas
  • Mobile phones must be on silent mode
  • No food or drinks allowed in the library
  • Return books on time to avoid fines
  • Handle books and equipment with care
  • Show your student ID when requested
  • Report any issues to library staff immediately

Booking Guidelines

  • Book seats at least 1 hour in advance
  • Maximum booking duration: 8 hours
  • Cancel bookings if you can't attend
  • Arrive within 15 minutes of booking time
  • One active booking per student at a time
  • Computer stations: 4-hour maximum per day
  • Report technical issues immediately
