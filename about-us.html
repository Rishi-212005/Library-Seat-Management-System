About District Library,Anantapur

Excellence in everything Since 1985

Our Mission

To provide quality higher education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for personal growth and societal contribution. We strive to create an inclusive learning environment that fosters critical thinking, innovation, and ethical leadership.

Our Vision

To be a premier government Library recognized for academic excellence, research innovation, and community engagement. We envision creating future leaders who will contribute meaningfully to society and drive positive change in their communities.

Our History

1985

Foundation

District Library,Anantapurwas established with the vision of providing accessible higher education to students from all backgrounds.

1995

Expansion

Added new departments and expanded infrastructure to accommodate growing student enrollment.

2005

Modernization

Introduced computer labs, digital library, and modern teaching facilities.

2020

Digital Transformation

Implemented online learning platforms and digital seat management system.
